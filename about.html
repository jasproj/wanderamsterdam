About WanderAmsterdam

We connect travelers with local canal-boat captains, walking-tour guides, food-tour operators, and small-group hosts across Amsterdam — direct to the operator, no booking-platform markup.

Our Mission

WanderAmsterdam started with a simple problem: the best operators in Amsterdam often get lost in noise. The Jordaan-based walking-tour guide running 12 people through the courtyards, the small-electric-boat canal cruise from Brouwersgracht, the De Pijp food tour at Albert Cuypmarkt — they often get buried under listings that pile booking fees and price markups on top of the operator's actual rate.

We built WanderAmsterdam to fix that. The platform indexes 500 verified tours across Amsterdam and links travelers directly to each operator's booking page. No markup on our end. No middleman fees stacked between you and the guide.

When you book through WanderAmsterdam, your money goes to the person actually running the tour. That's how tourism in Amsterdam is supposed to work.

What We Believe

The principles that guide everything we do

🚲

Local First

Every operator we list is based in Amsterdam or the Netherlands. No global tour packagers selling Amsterdam from offshore, no resorts-and-cruises aggregators posing as neighborhood guides.

💶

No Markup

Our links go to operator booking pages at operator prices. We earn a referral commission from completed bookings — it comes from the operator's marketing budget, not from your wallet.

🚢

Canal & Neighborhood Respect

We prioritize operators who run small groups, follow Amsterdam's anti-overtourism guidance, and don't crowd the same Anne Frank / Red Light District tour routes everyone else does.

📝

Real Listings

Tour descriptions come from the operator's actual booking page. No AI-generated marketing copy, no recycled stock blurbs.

📍

More Than the Ring of Canals

Centrum, Jordaan, De Pijp, Noord, Zuid, Oost — each Amsterdam area has its own character. We don't push you toward the same Dam-Square-and-Rijksmuseum loop.

🤝

Local Economy

Every booking through WanderAmsterdam is money flowing to a guide, a canal-boat captain, or a small food-tour operator. That's the point.

Transparency Matters

WanderAmsterdam is a discovery platform — not a booking site, not a tour operator. When you click Book Now, you're sent directly to the operator's booking system to pay them in EUR or USD on their terms.

We earn a referral commission from some bookings, which keeps the platform running. The commission comes out of the operator's existing marketing budget. It does not affect what you pay.

Affiliate Disclosure

WanderAmsterdam participates in referral programs with Amsterdam tour operators. When you book through our links, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This helps us maintain the platform and continue listing local operators. We only list operators we'd recommend to friends visiting Amsterdam.
