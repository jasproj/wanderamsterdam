Types of Zipline

🌳 Canopy Tours

Multi-line forest courses with platforms and short walks between. Half-day format.

⛰️ Mountain / High-line

Single long-run high-elevation lines. Higher height, faster speeds, single-trip format.

🎯 Adventure-park Combos

Zipline + high-rope / climbing-wall / freefall combined day pass.

Best Zipline Spots

Klimbos Veluwe (Klimrijk)

1 hour east

Forest adventure park with multiple zipline courses through tree canopy.

Outdoor Park de Eemhof

30 min east (Zeewolde)

Ziplines, climbing courses, and water park combined. Day-trip-friendly.

Klimpark Het Brabantse Land

1.5 hours south

Larger zipline circuits paired with high-ropes courses.

AdventurePark Almere

30 min east

Smaller zipline park near Amsterdam, family-friendly.

Klimbos Hoek van Holland

1 hour south (coast)

Coastal forest ziplines, often combined with North Sea beach day.

Snowworld Zoetermeer

45 min southwest

Indoor adventure complex with zipline, rope courses, and indoor skiing.

Zipline Tips

⚖️ Weight limits

Most operators set 250–270 lb max. Confirm before booking.

👕 Closed-toe shoes

Sandals and flip-flops are usually banned. Trail or athletic shoes work.

🤳 GoPro mount

Helmet-mounted cameras are usually allowed; chest mounts are sometimes restricted.

🌧️ Weather closures

Lightning and high winds shut courses fast. Book early in the day for the best chance.
