Types of Fishing

🎣 Inshore / Light Tackle

Half-day shallow-water fishing. Snapper, jacks, and reef species. Family-friendly.

🐟 Offshore / Deep Sea

Full-day blue-water trolling for marlin, mahi, tuna, wahoo. Bigger boats, longer runs.

🏞️ Freshwater / River

Trout, salmon, pike, or coarse fishing depending on region. Wading or boat-based.

Best Fishing Spots

Amstel River

Amsterdam

Carp and bream fishing with a free permit (Vispas). Multiple bank-fishing spots through the city.

IJsselmeer

North Holland

Pike, perch, and zander. Charter boats from Volendam and Enkhuizen.

Markermeer

North of Amsterdam

Pike and perch on charter boats. Calmer than IJsselmeer.

Sloterplas (city lake)

West Amsterdam

Urban fishing for perch, carp, bream. Vispas required.

North Sea (IJmuiden)

Coast

Day charters for sea bass, mackerel, and cod. 30 min from city centre.

Rivierenbuurt canals

South Amsterdam

Quieter canal fishing for residents and visitors with the Vispas permit.

Fishing Tips

🌅 Dawn departures

Most charters leave before sunrise. Plan your sleep accordingly.

🤢 Sea-sickness meds

Even half-day inshore can rough up — take meds the night before.

💵 Tip the mate

Crew is partly tip-funded. 15-20% of charter cost is standard.

🐟 Catch policy

Confirm catch-and-release vs. take-home before booking. Some species are protected.
